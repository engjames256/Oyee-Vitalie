Sales Promotion Application

A Sales Promotion Application that allows customers to enter a draw to win items by scanning a QR Code using their phones or submitting their information such as Receipt Number, Name, Outlet, Location as evidence of the purchase of the company products through an online form.

The Application also features an Admin area accessible by login where they can view, edit and delete items to be won, view all customers who have entered a draw to win specific items as well as spin to single out the lucky winner.
